Pass & play · All ages · No Adult prompts

Same Page

Everyone gets a secret place on a scale. Say one line that matches the theme about as much as your number — without naming it. Then put the group in order from lowest to highest and see how your values line up.

How it works

  1. Pick a category (Normal, Humor, Romance, Deep—or random), choose a question, and set how many people are playing.
  2. Each player peeks at their number alone (don’t show the screen to others).
  3. Everyone gives a one-line hint in turn.
  4. Hints are shuffled. Drag them into the order you think matches smallest feeling first, strongest last.
  5. Reveal and compare how close you were.

Game setup

You each get a unique number from 1 to 100. Higher = your answer leans further toward the “stronger” end of the question (more want, more trust, more regret — whatever fits).

Peek your number

Pass the device to —. Only that player should look at the screen.

Give your hint

— — one line that fits how strongly you feel at about your number’s level. Don’t say the number itself.

Sort the hints

Hints are shuffled. Drag cards or use arrows. Order from the weakest end of the scale (top) to the strongest (bottom).
